## 🧩 Project Overview

An interactive single-page Power BI dashboard built to analyze **operational revenue, gross margin, and profitability** across suppliers, sales teams, and product categories (Food & Drinks).

The dashboard gives management a clear view of which suppliers and sales channels are driving the most value — with dynamic slicers for **Month**, **Team**, and **Supplier**.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- What is the total operational revenue and gross margin across all suppliers?
- Which month generates the highest revenue and profit?
- Which supplier has the best gross margin percentage?
- Which sales channel (Retail / Wholesale / Online) contributes most to revenue?
- How does revenue split between Food and Drinks categories per team?

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📅 **November** is the strongest month for both revenue and gross margin
- 🏆 **Kappa Drinks** has the highest GM% at **56.38%** — most efficient supplier
- 🧾 **Two Brothers Mill** generates the highest revenue ($5.1M) but has the lowest margin (37.23%)
- 🛒 **Retail channel** dominates revenue, especially in the **Food** category ($3.9M)
- 🌐 **Online channel** contributes $2.0M — growth opportunity worth monitoring
